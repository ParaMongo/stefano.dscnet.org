In un contesto ISP, per quanto riguarda routing e core networking, ecco le differenze "vitali" tra IOS XE IPBASE e IOS XE ADVIPSERVICES.

* AToM
* BGP VPLS autodiscovery
* IPv6 routing:
  * MP-BGP
  * RIPv2
  * OSPFv3
  * static
* IPv6 tunneling
* Layer 2 ATM to ATM
* NAT
* PPP / PPPoE
* MPLS:
  * LDP / LDP autoconf. / LDP IGB sync.
  * TE
  * VPN
